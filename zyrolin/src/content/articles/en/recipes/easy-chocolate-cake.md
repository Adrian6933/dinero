This is the only chocolate cake recipe you'll ever need — one bowl, no special equipment, and impossible to mess up.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Flour | 1¾ cups |
| Sugar | 2 cups |
| Cocoa powder | ¾ cup |
| Baking soda | 1½ tsp |
| Eggs | 2 |
| Milk | 1 cup |
| Vegetable oil | ½ cup |
| Hot water | 1 cup |

## Step 1: Mix the dry ingredients

In one large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa, and baking soda with a pinch of salt.

## Step 2: Add the wet ingredients

Add the eggs, milk, and oil. Whisk until smooth.

## Step 3: Stir in hot water

Carefully stir in the hot water. The batter will be thin — that's exactly right and what makes the cake moist.

> Thin batter = moist cake. Don't be tempted to add more flour.

## Step 4: Bake

Pour into a greased pan and bake at 175°C (350°F) for 30–35 minutes, until a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.

## FAQ

**Can I make it without eggs?**
Yes — replace each egg with ¼ cup of unsweetened applesauce.

**How do I know it's done?**
A toothpick should come out with moist crumbs, not wet batter.

## Conclusion

One bowl, thin batter, 35 minutes. This easy chocolate cake is rich, moist, and nearly impossible to get wrong.
